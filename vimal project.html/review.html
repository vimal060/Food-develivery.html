Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent.
It is made with Indian spices, rice, and meat usually
that of chicken, goat, lamb, prawn, fish, and sometimes, in addition, eggs or vegetables such
as potatoes in certain regional varieties.
